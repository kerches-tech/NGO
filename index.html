Our Story

Educating Minds, Inspiring Hearts

WOMOVELAM SANITATION LBG was established in response to the growing threat of poor food and personal hygiene in Navrongo and its surrounding communities. These challenges, which are common across many parts of Ghana, have serious effects on public health and the environment.

Recognizing the urgent need for change, we began a journey to shift the narrative through positive action. Our aim is to improve hygiene and sanitation using simple scientific tools, community education, and strong advocacy.

Recognizing the urgent need for change, we began a journey to shift the narrative through positive action. Our aim is to improve hygiene and sanitation using simple scientific tools, community education, and strong advocacy.

We focus on promoting food safety, personal hygiene, and environmental protection by raising awareness and encouraging healthy behaviour. Our approach is grounded in practical, achievable programs that are designed to create lasting impact.

We believe that with education, community involvement, and consistent action, we can build a healthier and cleaner future for Navrongo and beyond.

Our Mission

We aim to make Navrongo the cleanest municipality within five years by using scientific communication tools.

Our Vision

We envision Navrongo becoming a model for behavioral change, leading communities in Ghana.

Core Values

Academic Excellence

We drive continuous public dialogue and policy influence to prioritize sanitation and hygiene at every level of society.

Community Engagement

We work hand-in-hand with communities, empowering individuals to take ownership of their health and environment.

Innovation

We apply simple yet effective scientific tools and creative strategies to promote lasting improvements in hygiene and sanitation.

Global Perspective

We approach local sanitation challenges with insights drawn from global best practices to ensure impactful, scalable solutions.
